Vision and Mission

Character

Character is the most important aspect of one's life. It is defined by Behaviour, Attitude, Ethics, and Qualities.


Competence

Competence is what makes you stand out in society. It includes Knowledge, Communication, Talent, and Skills.


Culture

Your Culture determines the boundaries in your life. It includes social behavior, institutions, beliefs, laws, customs, and habits that shape societies and individuals.

Activities Conducted

  • Career guidance and workshops by eminent personalities of respective domains.
  • Weekly sessions to build leadership qualities and communication skills.
  • Mentor meets to strengthen bonding with seniors, resolve doubts, and stay focused on personal goals.
