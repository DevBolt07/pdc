About the Club

We, the Members of Personality Development Club, work to develop our members' personalities by focusing on all three aspects: Character, Competence, and Culture. We refer to authentic resources like books and scientific articles to understand and apply physiological, philosophical, and cultural concepts.

Our team is committed to working together, helping each other, growing as a group, and contributing to society to the best of our abilities.

Vision and Mission

Character

Character is defined by behavior, attitude, ethics, and personal qualities. It is the foundation of one's identity.

Competence

Competence enables you to stand out in society. It includes knowledge, communication, talent, and skills.

Culture

Your culture determines the values and boundaries in your life. It covers social behavior, beliefs, laws, customs, and traditions of human societies.

Activities

  • Career guidance and workshops by eminent personalities.
  • Weekly sessions to develop leadership and communication skills.
  • Mentorship meets for bonding, doubt-solving, and goal alignment.
